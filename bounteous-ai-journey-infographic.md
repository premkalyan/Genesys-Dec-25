# Bounteous AI Maturity Journey
## From Enablement to Customer Delight

---

## Overview

A visual roadmap showing Bounteous's comprehensive AI capabilities across four stages of maturity. This demonstrates how we've built the full AI stack—from enabling our people to transforming customer experiences.

---

## The Journey (4 Stages - Left to Right)

### Stage 1: ENABLE
**Platform: Prism**
**Theme: AI Adoption & Training**
**Icon suggestion: Graduation cap or lightbulb**

Key Points:
- AI awareness definitions for every persona (executives to developers)
- Organization-wide usage tracking and adoption metrics
- Systematic training programs to uplift capabilities

Tagline: "You can't build with AI if your people don't understand AI"

---

### Stage 2: BUILD
**Platform: NEXUS**
**Theme: Agentic Operations**
**Icon suggestion: Robot or connected nodes**

Key Points:
- 4-Agent Architecture: Monitor → Verify → Execute → Learn
- Self-healing cross-system issue resolution
- Continuous learning and improvement

Results:
- 95% automation rate
- 15x faster resolution
- $50K/month cost savings

Tagline: "Our people build systems that operate themselves"

---

### Stage 3: ASSURE
**Platform: QIP (Quality Intelligence Platform)**
**Theme: Quality Intelligence**
**Icon suggestion: Checkmark shield or verification badge**

Key Points:
- Every PR verified, every finding tracked, every decision auditable
- Hybrid verification: 8 deterministic rules + 4 AI-powered checks
- Multi-framework compliance (CMMI, SOC 2, ISO 27001)
- Automated evidence collection from Git, Jira, Confluence

Tagline: "Compliance isn't a quarterly burden—it's continuous"

---

### Stage 4: DELIGHT
**Platform: Genesys AI Demos**
**Theme: Customer Experience**
**Icon suggestion: Heart with chat bubble or happy customer**

Key Points:
- Fine-tuned SLM: Domain-specific models trained on your data
- Smart Handoff: Zero-repetition bot-to-human transfer
- Real-time Sentiment: Dual-provider analysis (speed + accuracy)
- Historical Context: Full customer journey for agents

Tagline: "AI that understands your customer as well as your best agent"

## Visual Design Specifications

### Layout
- Horizontal timeline/roadmap format
- 4 stages connected by arrows or flowing line
- Each stage as a card or node with icon at top

### Color Scheme (suggestion)
- Stage 1 (Enable): Blue - represents learning/knowledge
- Stage 2 (Build): Purple - represents innovation/technology
- Stage 3 (Assure): Green - represents verification/success
- Stage 4 (Delight): Orange - represents warmth/customer focus

### Typography
- Main title: Bold, large
- Stage names: Bold, medium (ENABLE, BUILD, ASSURE, DELIGHT)
- Platform names: Medium weight (Prism, NEXUS, QIP, Genesys)
- Bullet points: Regular, smaller
