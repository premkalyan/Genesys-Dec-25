## Document 1: Premier Checking Account Agreement

### Section 4: Fee Schedule and Overdraft Policy

**4.1 Monthly Maintenance Fees**
The Bounteous Premier Checking account has a monthly maintenance fee of **$25.00**. This fee is waived if ANY of the following conditions are met during the monthly statement cycle:
1.  Average daily balance of **$15,000** or more in this account.
2.  Combined average daily balance of **$50,000** or more in linked qualifying Bounteous Bank accounts (Savings, Money Market, CD).
3.  A qualifying direct deposit of **$4,000** or more per statement cycle.

**4.2 Overdraft Services**
An overdraft occurs when you do not have enough money in your available balance to cover a transaction, but we pay it anyway.
*   **Standard Overdraft Practices:** We authorize and pay overdrafts for checks and automatic bill payments. We do NOT authorize and pay overdrafts for ATM transactions and everyday debit card transactions unless you opt-in.
*   **Overdraft Fees:** We charge a fee of **$34.00** each time we pay an overdraft.
*   **Daily Limit:** We will not charge you more than **three (3)** overdraft fees per day.
*   **De Minimis Rule:** We will not charge an overdraft fee if your account is overdrawn by **$5.00 or less** at the end of the business day.

**4.3 Foreign Transaction Fees**
For transactions made with your debit card in a foreign currency or outside the United States:
*   **International Transaction Fee:** **3%** of the US dollar amount of each transaction.
*   **Non-Bounteous ATM Fee (International):** **$5.00** per withdrawal, plus any fees charged by the ATM operator.

## Document 2: Wire Transfer Terms & Conditions

### Section 7: Cut-Off Times and Limits

**7.1 Domestic Wire Transfers**
*   **Cut-Off Time:** 4:00 PM Eastern Time (ET) on business days. Requests received after this time will be processed the next business day.
*   **Outgoing Fee:** **$30.00** per transfer.
*   **Incoming Fee:** **$15.00** per transfer.
*   **Daily Limit (Online):** $50,000. Higher limits available in-branch.

**7.2 International Wire Transfers**
*   **Cut-Off Time:** 5:00 PM Eastern Time (ET) for next-day processing.
*   **Outgoing Fee (US Dollars):** **$45.00** per transfer.
*   **Outgoing Fee (Foreign Currency):** **$0.00** (No fee for wires sent in foreign currency).
*   **Exchange Rates:** Bounteous Bank sets the exchange rate for foreign currency transfers, which includes a spread.

## Document 3: Mobile Deposit User Agreement

### Section 2: Funds Availability Policy

**2.1 General Policy**
Our policy is to make funds from your mobile deposits available to you on the **first business day** after the day we receive your deposit.

**2.2 Longer Delays May Apply**
Funds may be delayed for a longer period under the following circumstances:
*   You deposit checks totaling more than **$5,525** on any one day.
*   You redeposit a check that has been returned unpaid.
*   You have overdrawn your account repeatedly in the last six months.
*   There is an emergency, such as failure of computer or communications equipment.

We will notify you if we delay your ability to withdraw funds for any of these reasons, and we will tell you when the funds will be available. They will generally be available no later than the **seventh business day** after the day of your deposit.

**2.3 Special Rules for New Accounts**
If you are a new customer, the following special rules will apply during the first **30 days** your account is open:
*   Funds from electronic direct deposits to your account will be available on the day we receive the deposit.
*   Funds from deposits of cash, wire transfers, and the first **$5,525** of a day's total deposits of cashier's, certified, teller's, traveler's, and federal, state and local government checks will be available on the first business day after the day of your deposit if the deposit meets certain conditions.
*   The excess over **$5,525** will be available on the ninth business day after the day of your deposit.

## Document 4: Travel Notice Policy

### Section 9: Card Usage While Traveling

**9.1 Notification Requirements**
*   **Chip-Enabled Cards:** Customers with EMV chip-enabled debit or credit cards **do not** need to set a travel notice. Our advanced fraud detection systems automatically adjust to your location when the chip is used.
*   **Magnetic Stripe Only Cards:** Customers with older magnetic-stripe-only cards **must** notify the bank at least 24 hours prior to international travel to prevent blocked transactions.

In case of lost or stolen cards while abroad, call our international collect line at **+1-555-0199-2834**.
